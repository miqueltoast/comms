form

please make sure you agree with the TOS before submitting!

i only need basic contact information and a simple description + visual ref. i'll ask for further details once i contact you, so don't worry about being too specific ♡

process

openings: when i'm open for comms, i'll post a public form you can fill and submit!

slots: unless it's FCFS, i'll contact people whose submissions i've picked, usually within 24 hours;

request: we'll chat about what you have in mind, and i'll send you 2~3 pose thumbnails to pick from;

payment: i'll send a base sketch for review and, if accepted, the invoice;

updates: once paid, i'll send updates on the progress of the piece every step of the way, so you'll have plenty of chances for revisions;

files: when finished, i'll send you a web res JPG and a full res PNG of your piece(s);

queue: you can track my progress right in the above section! i usually finish them in order, but bigger orders may take longer.

tos

payment is due upfront! once sent, the invoice will be valid for up to 7 days;

payment plans are available for orders over $150;

cancelling may be done anytime - i will refund the amount equivalent to the steps not completed and retain a 40% fee.

refunds will be done in full if i don't complete the commission within 3 months of the last update, or a given deadline;

major changes must be requested at the base sketch stage. subsequent changes will be charged (max. 3/piece). minor changes are free but limited at my discretion;

personal use only. if you intend on making a profit with your piece, we can discuss!

reposting, printing and using your piece as pfp is ok! please include credits somewhere if reposting;

NSFW artwork is only available if you're 20+, as well as the characters i'll be portraying;

i may publish commissions for self-promotion purposes. if you want to keep your piece private, just let me know;

don't hesitate to ask if you have any questions or concerns!
